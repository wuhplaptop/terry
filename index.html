ABOUT THE KING

Terry "Tough Knuckles" Davis

Born and raised in the streets of Alliance, Ohio, Terry Davis earned his nickname "Tough Knuckles" through years of street fights and underground boxing matches where he never lost a bout.

Standing at 6'3" and weighing 245lbs of pure muscle, Terry commands respect wherever he goes. His signature move is the "Alliance Annihilator" - a devastating right hook that has knocked out countless challengers.

Beyond his fighting prowess, Terry is known for his charitable work in Alliance, funding youth centers and keeping the streets clean from crime. He's not just a fighter - he's a community leader.

THE RISE OF TOUGH KNUCKLES

1988 - Humble Beginnings

Born to a single mother in Alliance's east side, young Terry learned to fight early, protecting his neighborhood from bullies and drug dealers.

2005 - First Underground Fight

At 17, Terry entered his first illegal boxing match to pay his mother's medical bills. He knocked out a 250lb biker in 18 seconds.

2010 - The Nickname

After winning 25 straight fights with his bare knuckles (refusing gloves), the media dubbed him "Tough Knuckles" - a name that stuck.

2012 - Crowning Moment

Defeated the reigning "King of Alliance" in a legendary 12-round street fight. The defeated king handed Terry his crown and left town.

2018 - Rival Emerges

Brayden Powers, a cocky fighter from Youngstown, began challenging Terry's reign, leading to their first brutal encounter.

Present Day

Terry continues to rule Alliance with an iron fist, keeping the streets safe while preparing for his inevitable final showdown with Brayden Powers.

THE RIVAL: BRAYDEN POWERS

Brayden "The Powerhouse" Powers

Hailing from Youngstown, Brayden Powers represents everything Terry stands against. A mercenary fighter with no code, Brayden has made it his mission to dethrone Terry and claim Alliance for himself.

Their rivalry began in 2018 when Brayden interrupted one of Terry's charity events, leading to their first brutal confrontation that ended in a draw after police intervention.

Since then, Brayden has been Terry's only opponent to survive a full fight, making him the most dangerous threat to Terry's reign.

Notable Confrontations

  • 2018 Charity Event: First encounter, ended in draw
  • 2019 Street Fight: Terry won by TKO in 9th round
  • 2021 Parking Lot Brawl: Most brutal fight yet, no winner declared
